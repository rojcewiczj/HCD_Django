Weatherization Assistance Program

Provided by Housing and Community Development

WAP is a program designed to assist low-income households in reducing their fuel costs while contributing to national energy conservation through increased energy efficiency and consumer education. Weatherization work can include various types of insulation, air sealing, duct sealing,

Why its recommended

Your home has a high energy burden

Eligibility

You rent or own a home in Shelby County

Your yearly household income is less than $XX,000 for a family of X

Your home does not have any serious structural issues

Your home does not have any known lead hazards

What You Need to Apply

Government issued ID

Proof of income for the past 3 months

Energy bills for the past 3 months

Proof of homeownership or residence

What to Expect Before Applying

The City has 90 days to approve or deny the application. If your application is approved, a certified auditor will visit your home to perform an energy audit resulting in a work order with a list of recommended weatherization measures. However, please note that due to high demand, priority will be given to persons with disability, and the elderly. In addition, homes may be deferred due to poor structural conditions.
